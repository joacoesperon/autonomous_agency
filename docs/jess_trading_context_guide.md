🎨 Jess Trading – Brand Identity
1. Color Palette
Carbon Black
HEX: #101010 → hacia afuera #010101
RGB centro: (10, 10, 10) → exterior: (1, 1, 1)
Uso: Main background con degradado radial desde un gris muy oscuro en el centro hacia negro puro en el exterior. Da un look premium, profundo y moderno.
Aplicación: fondos, hero sections, carruseles, stories.
Neon Green
HEX base: #45B14F
RGB variante luminosa: (165, 242, 140) → HEX: #A5F28C
Uso: Candle color + key highlights. Simboliza crecimiento, profit y automatización.
Aplicación: números de PnL, títulos impactantes, highlights de gráficos.
Light Gray
HEX base: #A7A7A7
RGB variante clara: (215, 215, 215) → HEX: #D7D7D7
Uso: Secondary text. Contrasta suavemente con el fondo oscuro.
Aplicación: subtítulos, disclaimers, body copy.
Electric Blue
HEX: #2979FF
RGB: (41, 121, 255)
Uso: Accent color para guiar acciones. Solo en CTAs y links.
Aplicación: botones, “link in bio”, call-to-actions.

2. Usage Rule
Mantener Carbon Black como dominante (siempre con degradado radial).
Usar Neon Green solo en elementos clave (resultados, porcentajes, keywords).
Light Gray para mantener legibilidad en textos secundarios.
Electric Blue de manera controlada, reservado a CTAs → garantiza contraste y dirección visual.

2. Typography
Primary Font: Inter (Google Font).
Weights:
Bold → Titles, headlines, CTAs.
Regular/Medium → Body text, captions, descriptions.
Light → Subtle elements, secondary captions.
Usage Rule: Keep text minimal and highly legible. Never use more than two weights per design. Always prioritize clarity on mobile screens.

3. Logo Usage
Primary Logo: Jess Trading wordmark with symbol (robot/graph hybrid).
Formats: Horizontal (for headers, banners) and Square (for profile pics, app icons).
Safe Space: Maintain clear space equal to the height of the “J” around the logo.
Backgrounds: Prefer Carbon Black. Use white version of the logo only on dark photos or gradients.
Don’t:
Don’t stretch or distort the logo.
Don’t add drop shadows or glows.
Don’t use colors outside the official palette.

4. Brand Voice & Tone
Tone: Premium, confident, but approachable.
Voice: Clear, concise, free of hype.
Personality: Professional fintech + visionary innovator.
Promise: “The future of trading is automated.”
Examples:
✅ “Automate your trades. Unlock your time.”
❌ “Get rich overnight with this secret hack!”

5. Visual Style
Aesthetic: Minimalist fintech. Inspired by Apple keynotes and premium SaaS brands.
Icons: Flat, vector-style, simple and modern.
Layouts: Grid-based, lots of negative space, strong contrast.
Imagery: Candlestick charts, abstract tech visuals, finance-related graphics. Always keep it clean and premium — avoid stocky or cartoonish styles.
